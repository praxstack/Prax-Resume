S D E   II   — at Amazon Travel · Hotels, Flights & Insurance

JUL 2023  ·  SEP 2025  ·  BENGALURU
Owner of four Java microservices across the booking funnel; principal engineer on the pricing, payments, deployment, and on-call fronts of a global traveler platform.
  • THE PRICING FAN-OUT. Re-architected the traveler's pricing call upon CompletableFuture.allOf with bulkhead ExecutorService pools — cut p90 from 1.10 s to 0.59 s (−47%) and lifted traveler conversion by +33%.
  • THE IDEMPOTENT LEDGER. Wrote a payment webhook upon SQS and Step Functions, keyed by DynamoDB dedupe entries — reclaimed −43 bps CPT and silenced the double-capture incidents for good.
  • THE CDK REWRITE. Rewrote six legacy pipelines in Java CDK — halved rollbacks, and pulled MTTR down by a full day.
  • THE SILENCED PAGER. Dragged the weekly on-call from 20+ pages to ~5, config-hygiene from 5–6 → 0 through automated guardrails; resolved 150+ incidents, and stopped ~30 bps of funnel-churn on insurance attach.
