Why This Beats the Form

  1. The artifacts are the moat. Anyone can fill a Google form. Almost no candidates will show up with a Java agentic engine they shipped this month, with idempotency keys and an audit log because that’s how a real FDSE thinks about reliability.
  2. The form filters on year, not on signal. Year-2022 MCA → auto-filter. Year-2022 MCA + Loom + repo + ex-Amazon DM → recruiter override.
  3. Honest disclosure beats clever year-picking. “Form didn’t have my year, here’s what I picked and why” reads as a careful candidate. Picking 2025 reads as someone who’ll cut corners on customer projects.
